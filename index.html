About Us

Usman Engineering & Co started its operations in 2000 with a small setup, starting with Building wiring, Air Conditioning and Plumber Services. After an untiring journey of more than one and half decade, we are now providing full range of building maintenance and engineering services to hundreds of corporate (National & Multi-national), expats and domestic clients. Our experience at the level includes everything and anything to successfully build, renovate and maintain homes or business premises. Due to high demand for a reliable, trustworthy and a multi-functional company, we offer a full range of building maintenance services required in any home or office.

The future vision of the company is to continue to ensure excellence and quality in its services with high standards of workmanship. Usman Engineering & Co also committed to serve humanity by supporting the local workforce, by providing jobs and on job trainings in areas that are experiencing skill shortages. The company is currently training its staff through professional trainers and is looking forward to achieving higher standards in the years to come.

Our Services

CONSTRUCTION

Our teams are committed to providing durable and cost effective projects. Our major area of expertise lies in renovation. We use high quality material and innovative procedures to transform your old fashioned commercial buildings, giving them a modern and contemporary look.

SOLAR

Our SOLAR services include provision, installation, repairing, maintenance of solar power systems. our team specializes in solar repair and maintenance. Our mission is to help you stay energy independent. We are the Solar Service Professionals.

AC

Our AC services include provision, installation, repairing, gas filling,servicing. ELECTRICIAN

We strongly believe in providing human safety in this field. Our electricians are professional, highly skilled and well aware of safety measures and value of human life.We can do maintenance & repair work as well as tasks related to new construction such as wiring and installation of fixtures, lights, generators and UPS etc.

PLUMBER

Our Plumbers are skilled, trained and professional. We can do maintenance & repair work as well as tasks related to new construction such as piping (PPR, PVC, GI or rubber) and installation of water heaters, geysers, water motors, pressure pumps, toilet seats etc. So whether you are building or constructing a house, office, or a commercial plaza, UE is ready to service you.

CARPENTER

Our carpentry services include provision of beds, furniture, doors, windows, cupboards, cabinets, drawers, shelves, racks, stair railings, partitions, work stations, front desks, security guard cabins and much more. Most of the times we provided furniture to our foreigner clients as per designs of worldwide leading furniture manufacturers.

PAINTER

Our painting service is provided through partners who are skilled, and professional. We can do paint work related to new construction as well as maintenance (such as white wash). Wall paint services are offered Either you can pay for labour only and provide your own paint / materials OR Pay for both the labour service and paint / material (which we will provide).

WELDER

Our welder services include metal welding, aluminum welding, stainless steel welding and fabrication services.
